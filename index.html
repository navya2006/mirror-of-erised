MIRROR OF ERISED

Presenting the all new AR based virtual fitting solution with a real-time try-on solution. By using a smart mirror, customers can try a dress, top, or any other piece of clothing on their virtual models displayed on the mirror.

It serves the purpose of a virtual fitting room. The mirror scans the image of the user to create a virtual model wearing that scanned piece of clothing.
